UCI Classification Guide

This page explains how UCI race classifications work for both men’s and women’s professional cycling.

Prefix Codes

  • 1.X: One-day race (e.g., Paris–Roubaix)
  • 2.X: Multi-day (stage) race (e.g., Tour de France)

Classification

  • X.UWT: Men's World Tour
  • X.WWT: Women's World Tour
  • X.Pro: ProSeries – max 70% Men's WorldTeams, min 4 Women's WorldTeams
  • X.1: Max 50% Men's WorldTeams, 1–7 Women's WorldTeams
  • X.2: No Men's WorldTeams, up to 3 Women's WorldTeams

Suffix Codes

  • XXU: Under 23 (U23) race
  • XXNcup: Nations Cup – national or mixed teams

Other Classifications

  • WC: World Championships
  • CC: Continental Championships
  • NC: National Championships
  • JR: Regional Games

Examples

  • 1.UWT: Paris–Roubaix
  • 2.UWT: Tour de France
  • 1.WWT: Strade Bianche Donne
  • 2.WWT: Tour de France Femmes
  • 1.Pro: Paris–Tours
  • 2.Pro: Tour de Langkawi
  • 1.1: Le Samyn
  • 2.1: Tour of Slovakia
  • 1.2: Paris–Troyes
  • 2.2: Tour de Normandie
