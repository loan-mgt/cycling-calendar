Race Categories Guide

This page explains the category codes used in our calendar to organize races.

Race Categories

The calendar uses the following category codes to organize races:

Professional Road

  • ME: Men Elite - Top tier professional men's road racing
  • WE: Women Elite - Top tier professional women's road racing

Disciplines

  • track: Track Cycling - Velodrome events
  • MTB: Mountain Biking - Cross-country and downhill events

Championships & Development

  • NC: National Championships - Country-specific championship races
  • WC: World Championships - UCI World Championship events
  • JR: Junior - Races for under-19 or development categories

Examples

  • ME: Tour de France, Paris-Roubaix
  • WE: Tour de France Femmes, Giro d'Italia Women
  • track: European Track Championships
  • MTB: UCI MTB World Cup
